Our Services

We Lift You Up

Elevator And Escalator Spares Manufacturing

Elevator And Escalator Spares Manufacturing is a service that specializes in the design, fabrication, and installation of spare parts and components to repair, maintain, and improve elevators and escalators. We offer quality products and reliable service to ensure the smooth and safe operation of all types of elevators and escalators.

New Lift Installation/ Dismantleing

New Lift Installation/ Dismantleing is a service that provides professional installation and dismantle of elevators in homes, businesses and other structures. Our experienced technicians ensure that the installation and dismantle process is done safely and efficiently, ensuring that the elevator is correctly and securely installed

Elevator Enhancement
Services

Elevator Enhancement Services provide comprehensive solutions for elevators and escalators, from modernizations and repairs to maintenance and preventive services. Our experienced professionals are committed to providing the most reliable, safe and cost-effective services.

Modernization

Modernization provides comprehensive and reliable elevator maintenance and repair services, as well as modernization services to ensure your elevators are up to modern safety standards.

Elevator Consultation and Analysis

Elevator Consultation and Analysis provides expert advice and service to ensure that elevators are operating safely and efficiently. Our experienced staff will inspect, analyze, and provide tailored solutions to maximize the lifespan and performance of your elevator.

AMC/One-Time
Service

AMC/One-Time Service is a specialized service that provides a single, comprehensive solution for a variety of IT needs. It covers a wide range of tasks from installation and setup of hardware and software to maintenance, troubleshooting, and repair. This service is designed to offer a complete and efficient solution for any IT-related requirements.

Lift licensing services New/ renewal

Lift Licensing Services New/ Renewal provides an efficient, cost-effective and streamlined approach to obtaining or renewing lift licences. Our team of experienced professionals will guide you through the necessary steps, ensuring that you have the necessary documents and information needed to ensure your lift is compliant with all relevant regulations.
